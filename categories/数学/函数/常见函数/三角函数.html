2020-09-09|数学函数常见函数

辅助角公式

证明：

\begin{align*}
& a\sin x+b\cos x \
= &\sqrt{a2+b2}(\frac{a}{\sqrt{a2+b2}}\sin x+\frac{b}{\sqrt{a2+b2}}\cos x) \
= &\sqrt{a2+b2}(\cos\varphi\sin x+\sin\varphi\cos x) \
= &\sqrt{a2+b2}\sin(x+\varphi) \
= &\sqrt{a2+b2}\sin(x+\arctan(\frac{\sin\varphi}{\cos\varphi})) \
= &\sqrt{a2+b2}\sin(x+\arctan\frac{b}{a})
\end{align*}
